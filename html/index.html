TaylorMade Solutions

TaylorMade Solutions, LLC is a commercial cleaning and facilities maintenance services provider. We are based in Prince George’s County and certified by the State of Maryland as a Minority Business Enterprise and a Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business Enterprise.

We distinguish ourselves with an exceptional combination of industry expertise and knowledge, gained through many years’ experience from the military and private sector perspectives. We offer a full range of behind the scenes facility support services to anable your facility to funtion properly and bring out the best in your establishment.
